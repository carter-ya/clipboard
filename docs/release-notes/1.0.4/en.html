Clipboard 1.0.4

Fixes

  • The global shortcut now reliably brings up the window — it no longer occasionally misses the first press after the app has been idle.
  • The history cap and maximum-size fields in Settings are now clickable across the whole field, with a steady text cursor and no accidental select-all on click.
  • The Settings window now stays where you place it and no longer jumps on reopen.
